My SEO Process

How I plan and manage the entire SEO process for my client

1

Research

  • Keyword research, competitor analysis, and market insights.
  • Identify high-potential keywords based on search volume and intent.
  • Analyze competitors’ strengths, weaknesses, and backlink profiles.
  • Study market trends and audience behavior to uncover growth opportunities.
2

Strategy

  • Build a tailored roadmap for SEO visibility and conversions.
  • Define clear SEO goals aligned with client business objectives.
  • Develop a content plan that targets buyer journey stages.
  • Prioritize technical, on-page, and off-page improvements.
3

Execution

  • Optimize content, fix technical issues, and build authority.
  • Implement on-page optimizations (meta tags, headings, schema, internal linking).
  • Enhance user experience with fast loading, mobile-friendliness, and secure browsing.
  • Build domain authority through ethical link-building and outreach campaigns.
4

Reporting

  • Transparent performance reporting with growth insights.
  • Provide regular SEO reports with rankings, traffic, and conversions.
  • Highlight key wins, areas of improvement, and actionable next steps.
  • Continuously refine strategy based on data-driven results.
